📖 Help Guide

Everything you need to know to build the perfect zoo!

🎮 Getting Started

Welcome to Zoo Tycoon! Here's how to get started:

Step 1: Build Your First Exhibit

Click the "🏗 Build Exhibit ($1000)" button at the top. Each exhibit can house multiple animals.

Step 2: Buy Animals

Go to the Shop tab and click "Add to Zoo" on any animal. Choose which exhibit to place them in and their gender.

Step 3: Build Amenities

Visit the Amenities tab to build food stands, gift shops, benches, and restrooms. These keep visitors happy and generate extra income!

Step 4: Advance Days

Click "⏭ End Day" to progress time. You'll earn money from ticket sales and amenities, but pay for animal upkeep and staff salaries.

🐾 Animal Happiness

Happy animals attract more visitors and can breed! Here's what affects happiness:

  • Population: Each species has a minimum and maximum number. Too few = lonely, too many = overcrowded.
  • Preferred Upgrades: Animals like certain shelters, decorations, and facilities. Check the animal's info before buying!
  • Staff: Hire keepers and janitors to boost animal happiness.
💡 Tip: Keep animal happiness at 100% to enable breeding!

👥 Visitor Satisfaction

Happy visitors spend more money! Build amenities to keep them satisfied:

  • Benches: Tired visitors need somewhere to sit
  • Restrooms: Long lines = unhappy visitors
  • Food Stands: Hungry visitors will leave to find food elsewhere
  • Gift Shops: Visitors love to buy souvenirs!
⚠️ Warning: Check the Visitors tab to see complaints and fix problems quickly!

💰 Money Management

Income Sources:

  • Ticket Sales: Based on number of visitors and ticket price
  • Animal Income: Some animals generate daily income
  • Amenity Sales: Food stands and gift shops earn money from visitors

Expenses:

  • Animal Upkeep: $2 + 15% of animal's daily income
  • Staff Salaries: Paid daily for each hired staff member
  • Exhibit Maintenance: $5 per exhibit per day
  • Amenity Maintenance: Varies by amenity type
💡 Tip: Don't hire too many staff early on - their salaries add up quickly!

🍼 Breeding

Breeding animals creates babies and boosts visitor excitement!

Requirements:

  • At least 2 animals of the same species in one exhibit
  • At least 1 male and 1 female
  • 100% animal happiness
  • Luck! (Each species has a base breeding chance)

Boost Breeding:

  • Hire veterinarians for a breeding bonus
  • Keep animals perfectly happy
  • Add preferred upgrades to exhibits

👷 Staff

Staff members provide bonuses to your zoo:

  • Zoo Keepers: Boost animal happiness and cleanliness
  • Veterinarians: Increase breeding success rate
  • Janitors: Reduce visitor complaints
  • Tour Guides: Increase visitor satisfaction
⚠️ Warning: Each staff member has a maximum hire limit. Check their card for details!

🏆 Achievements

Complete achievements to unlock rare animals and bragging rights!

Popular Achievements:

  • First Animal: Buy your first animal
  • Animal Collector: Own 10 animals
  • First Profit: Reach $10,000
  • Zoo Mogul: Reach $100,000
  • New Arrival: Breed your first animal

Check the Achievements tab to see your progress!

📊 Reports

The Reports tab shows your zoo's performance over time:

  • Income Graph: Track your monthly earnings
  • Visitor Trends: See how many visitors you're attracting
  • Monthly Stats: Detailed breakdown of each month

🎮 Advanced Tips

  • Start Small: Begin with cheap animals like chickens and pygmy goats
  • Balance Exhibits: Don't put carnivores with herbivores!
  • Save Money: Keep a cash reserve for unexpected expenses
  • Upgrade Wisely: Only buy upgrades that animals actually prefer
  • Monitor Visitors: Check the Visitors tab daily for complaints
  • Breed Strategically: Focus on high-income animals first

❓ Troubleshooting

My animals won't breed!

  • Make sure you have both males and females
  • Check that happiness is at 100%
  • Verify you have the minimum number of animals
  • Breeding is random - keep trying!

I'm losing money!

  • Build more amenities to increase revenue
  • Reduce staff if you can't afford salaries
  • Add more animals to attract visitors
  • Check your animal upkeep costs

Visitors are complaining!

  • Build more benches for seating
  • Add more restrooms
  • Build food stands
  • Hire janitors to reduce complaints
